the following is the information about telugu language

Telugu has an unbroken, prolific, and diverse literary tradition of over a thousand years.[31][32] Pavuluri Mallana's Sāra Sangraha Ganitamu (c. 11th century) is the first scientific treatise on mathematics in any Dravidian language.[33][34] Avadhānaṃ, a literary performance that requires immense memory power and an in-depth knowledge of literature and prosody, originated and was specially cultivated among Telugu poets for over five centuries.[35][36] Roughly 10,000 pre-colonial inscriptions exist in Telugu
